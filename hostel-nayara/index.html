About Nayara Ladies Hostel

Located in the heart of Vellore, Nayara Ladies Hostel offers safe, clean and supportive accommodation tailored for students and working women. We prioritize security, hygiene and a homelike atmosphere — all at affordable rates. Our campus-friendly location provides easy access to colleges, hospitals and public transport.

We provide well-furnished rooms, nutritious home-style meals, daily cleaning, and round-the-clock assistance. Join our friendly community that values privacy, respect and wellbeing.
